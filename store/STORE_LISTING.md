## Description
```
Golf Skins is the simplest way to run a skins game with your friends.

Create a game in seconds, share a code, and everyone joins from their own phone.
Tap a winner on each hole — or mark it a tie and watch the skin carry over to the
next. The leaderboard and standings update live for everyone in the group.

When the round ends, Golf Skins does the math for you: it shows the final skins,
each player's net, and a clean "who owes whom" summary so you can settle up
however you like.

FEATURES
• Create or join games with a simple code
• 9 or 18 holes, with skins carryover on ties
• Live scoring synced across every player's phone
• Change a hole result with the other player's approval — no arguments
• Final standings and an automatic settle-up summary
• Play as a guest, or sign up to save your match history

Golf Skins is a scorekeeper for friendly games. It does not handle payments or
wagers — players settle up among themselves.
```
